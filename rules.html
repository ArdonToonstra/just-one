Objective

Just One is a cooperative party game where all players work together to achieve the best score! Your mission is to help one player guess a mystery word by writing unique clues.

Key Rule: Be unique! Identical clues are canceled out.

Setup

1 Draw 13 cards to form a face-down deck
2 Each player gets an easel and marker
3 Choose the first guesser

Gameplay

1
Draw & Choose

Active player draws a card and picks a number (1-5). This is the mystery word!

2
Write Clues

Other players secretly write one-word clues on their easels.

3
Eyes Closed

Guesser closes eyes while others reveal clues to each other.

4
Cancel Duplicates

Remove all identical clues (e.g., two "Movie" clues = both canceled).

5
Guess Time!

Guesser opens eyes and tries to guess using remaining clues.
