Objective

Just One is a cooperative party game. All players play together to get the best score! Together, you make one player guess a Mystery word by secretly writing a clue. Choose your clue without coordinating with each other and be unique, because all identical clues will be canceled!

Setup

  • Shuffle the cards and draw 13 to form a face-down deck.
  • Each player takes an easel and a marker.
  • Choose a player to be the first guesser.

Gameplay (per round)

  1. The active player draws a card and chooses a number from 1 to 5. This is the Mystery word for the round.
  2. The other players secretly write a one-word clue on their easels.
  3. Once everyone has written their clue, the active player closes their eyes. All other players reveal their clues to each other.
  4. All identical clues are canceled. For example, if two players write "Movie", both clues are canceled.
  5. The active player opens their eyes and tries to guess the Mystery word with the remaining clues.

Guessing

  • Correct Guess: If the guess is correct, the card is added to the score pile.
  • Incorrect Guess: If the guess is incorrect, the current card and the top card of the deck are discarded.
  • Pass: The guesser can choose to pass. In this case, only the current card is discarded.

End of the Game

The game ends when the deck is empty. The final score is the number of correctly guessed cards.
